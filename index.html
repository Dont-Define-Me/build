Why should you choose Don't Define Me™?

Made from scratch.

We believe you are unique — and because of that, your order is unique.

Which is why each order is hand made in our studio
and printed on 100% Preshrunk Ultra Soft Cotton to provide optimum comfiness and durability.


Jake Pavek, who has cerebral palsy developed this brand entirely from scratch beginning in 2017
and continues to build towards his goal of a better, more inclusive world.

Over 1500+ hours have been dedicated to building Don't Define Me™ into what it is today.
You are guarenteed that the same attention and thought that went into creating this brand
will also be put into creating your shirt.

Live a better life.

The idea of Don't Define Me™ is two-fold.

First, you must avoid defining, or putting a label on, someone you do not know.
It doesn't matter how they look, walk, or talk.. until you have a conservation with them,
they are just like you & I: Undefinable.™

Second, you must challenge yourself to be the best possible version of yourself every single day.
You will find that by not being judgemental of other people and motivating yourself to be great,
and do great things,
you will live a happier, more fulfilling life.

We have been living an Undefinable™ lifestyle for the last 6 years
and can personally vouch that it will change your life.

Make a statement.

Use this platform to make a statement about yourself in a way that has never been done before.
Whether it is towards a boss, bully, co-worker, family member, friend, a total stranger, or the entire world...

By becoming Undefinable, you are telling everyone that you refuse to be defined
and you are more than just a label that society has put on you.
You are telling the world to get to know you first before placing judgement on you.

The statement you make will be sure to grab the attention of those around you.

A promise to yourself and others.

By wearing a Don't Define Me™ shirt and representing the brand,
you are making a promise to yourself that you won't be defined based on your
beliefs, condition, disability, looks, gender, hobbies, sexual orientation, etc.

At the same time, you are making a promise to others not to define them.

With the mindset of not putting labels on individuals without getting to know them first,
we are creating a better, more inclusive world
for ourselves and the people we love.
